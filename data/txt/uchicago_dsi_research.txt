Overview:
Research at the Data Science Institute addresses important scientific and societal questions through coordinated advances in applications, models, algorithms, and platforms.

Research Initiatives:
1. AI + Science
   - A new paradigm of transformational AI-enabled scientific discovery across the physical and biological sciences, advancing core AI principles and training a new generation of diverse interdisciplinary scientists.

2. Data & Democracy
   - Research at the intersection of computation and democratic representation.
   - Focuses on how data science innovations such as better mechanisms, models, and metrics can strengthen democracy.

3. AICE: AI for Climate
   - A program of the Institute for Climate and Sustainable Growth and DSI.
   - Aims to accelerate and transform climate research with a focus on both scientific advances and societal impacts.

4. Data Ecology
   - Studies dataflows and designs interventions to control how data impacts the world.

5. Novel Intelligence
   - Explores how to train AI models that augment rather than mimic human capacity.
