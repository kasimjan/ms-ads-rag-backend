Visible biography/details parsed from the page:

Greg Green:
Greg Green is listed as Senior Instructional Professor and Director of the MS in Applied Data Science Program at the University of Chicago, and Senior Director for Industrial Partnerships and Strategy at the Data Science Institute. The page says he focuses on helping professional data science students solve complex industry problems with greater impact. It also notes that he has launched new offerings such as Leadership in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, Consulting in Data Science, and Your Career in Data Science. His prior industry roles include Chief Analytics Officer at Harland Clarke Holdings, Director at Google, EVP/Managing Director at Publicis Groupe, and Analytics Practice Lead at PwC. He holds a PhD in Mathematics from Claremont Graduate School and an MS in Statistics from Michigan State University.

Arnab Bose:
Arnab Bose is listed as Associate Senior Instructional Professor, Faculty Director of the online program, and Curriculum Director in the MS in Applied Data Science program. The page says he has more than two decades of industry experience. It notes that he previously founded an AI startup focused on industrial applications in healthcare, finance, semiconductor, and retail, and later exited after acquisition. He is also listed as Chief Scientific Officer at UST AlphaAI. At UChicago he teaches Machine Learning, Machine Learning Operations, Time Series Analysis and Forecasting, and Data Science in Healthcare. He holds MS and PhD degrees from the University of Southern California and a BTech from IIT Kharagpur.

Francisco Azeredo:
Francisco Azeredo is described as a financial economist and decision scientist with more than 15 years of experience in leading financial and consulting firms in the Chicago area. The page says he previously served as senior vice president at Northern Trust, working on research and development of quantitative strategies for fixed income and equities. His teaching areas in the MS-ADS program include reinforcement learning, statistical analysis, non-linear statistical modeling, and optimization methods. He holds a PhD in Economics from the University of California, Santa Barbara.

Anil D. Chaturvedi:
The page says Anil Chaturvedi has more than 35 years of professional experience, including roles at AT&T Bell Labs, Kraft Foods, Capital One, and Accenture, and consulting work for Bank of America, Fannie Mae, Johnson & Johnson, and Procter & Gamble. His research interests include enhancing business value using data science. It also says he has patented and published algorithms for predictive modeling, market segmentation, product development, customer loyalty, promotion optimization, and brand strategy. He earned a PhD from Rutgers University and an MBA from IIM Ahmedabad.

Nick Kadochnikov:
Nick Kadochnikov is listed as Associate Clinical Professor at the University of Chicago Data Science Institute and head of Artificial Intelligence and Advanced Technology at Harbor Global. The page says he previously served as Director of AI at William Blair and spent 20 years at IBM developing AI solutions across supply chain, sales, marketing, finance, procurement, legal, and healthcare.

Selected additional visible entries:
- Ignas Grabauskas: Data Scientist at Simpson Thacher; the page says his recent work includes LLM applications such as document classification, custom named-entity recognition, and summarization. He holds a BA in Physics from the University of Chicago and is an alumnus of the MS in Applied Data Science program.
- Batu Gundogdu: Holds a PhD in Electrical Engineering and has 15+ years of deep-learning experience across speech, language, and medical imaging. The page says he teaches Bayesian Machine Learning for Generative AI and Advanced Linear Algebra for Machine Learning.
- Justin Kurland: Vice President and Tech Fellow at Goldman Sachs with 20+ years in data and analytics, 50+ peer-reviewed publications, and open-source contributions in machine learning and time series forecasting.
- Jeong-Yoon Lee: Senior Manager in Applied Science at Uber; the page says he leads work in personalization, targeting, optimization, and causal inference for marketing, and is involved with the open-source CausalML project.
- John Navarro: Adjunct Instructor and CFO at John Burns Construction Company; previously worked at McKinsey, Accenture, and 84.51°. He holds both a BA in Economics and an MS in Applied Data Science from the University of Chicago.
- Danny Ng: Director of Machine Learning at Expedia Group and Adjunct Lecturer at the University of Chicago. The page says he teaches graduate courses in the MS in Applied Data Science program and earned his PhD in Statistics from the University of Chicago.
