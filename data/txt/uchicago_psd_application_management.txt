Application instructions:
- Use the “Start New Application” / “Create an account” link to begin a new application.
- If you plan to apply to more than one program, begin a separate application for each program.
- If you have questions about your application, contact the specific program you are applying to.
- Program contact information is available on the Graduate Admissions website.

Non-Degree Visiting Student Status (NDVS):
- NDVS is available to advanced graduate and undergraduate students pursuing a degree at another institution who have been invited by a University of Chicago faculty member to temporarily work on that faculty member’s research project.
- This status also applies to undergraduate students participating in summer research opportunities such as the PKU-CCME exchange program or other research experiences for undergraduates (REUs).
- Questions about NDVS status may be directed to Aleksandra Ninova-Parris at aninovaparris@uchicago.edu

Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago (TTIC):
- TTIC students participating in research or University of Chicago courses should complete a non-degree application on this site to establish a student record with the University of Chicago.

Data use / privacy:
- Application data may be used to render admissions decisions, for internal and external reporting, institutional research, and other purposes.
- Applicants are directed to review the University Privacy Notice for more information.

Returning users:
